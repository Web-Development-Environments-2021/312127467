Hi there! My name is Peter Griffin and I live in Quahog, Rhode Island.
I am a fictional character taking part in a televison show called "Family Guy" and voiced by Seth MacFarlane.
My wife's name is Louis and together we have a dog, Brian, and three funny, creative and unique children: Chris, Meg and Stewie.
Most importantly, I have the best job one could ask for - working in the brewery. In my free time I like to do crazy things!
From drinking beer straight from the barrel using a forklift to buying a helicopter and fighting my chicken nemesis.
I love hanging out with my family, watching televison and having fun. I love going on wild adventures
with my dog and ofcourse drinking at the "Clam"! Have a crazy adventure in mind? IM THERE!
